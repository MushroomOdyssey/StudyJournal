A relative link is very different. It points to a familiar location that's easily within reach. You just need some basic directions to get you pointed in the right direction.

Instead of a starting location of "the whole world" and narrowing it down, you start from wherever you happen to be when you ask the question.
FOOLE: Excuse me noble sire, where might I find the privy?
NOBLE: "Down the hall. Left past the second suite of armor."


In other words, they are instructions that might change relative to your current location.
